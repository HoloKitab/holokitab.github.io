About Us

HoloKitab is an early starge EdTech startup based in Punjab. Our mission is to make learning more interactive and engaging by using textbooks. We believe that textbooks are the best way to understand and retain information for a longer period, as compared to videos which are a passive way of learning. So, we are using Augmented Reality, to make text books more interesting for students.

Welcome!

Augmented reality makes virtual objects "belong" to the real world. Using the technology, students can interatct with the 3D models as if they were real. This helps in the student's learning process tremendoudly, as it gives a 360 degree understanding of a concept.

Our Vision

While we are moving towards video-enabled learning platforms, what we are loosing in this process is creativity and brainstorming. Using this technolgy will enable your child to maximize his/her ability to spend their time learning curricular subjects while minimizing the time spent learning how to use the new tech. Learning using visuals in the real-world can also inspire empathy in an individual.

The technology offers three-dimensional methods of visualizing vs the tranditional one-dimension. The combination of interactivity and engagement with emotions can enhance the ability of your children to remeber what they have learned and leading to faster acquistion of information and skills.

Philosophy & Mission

We are living in a world where technology is evolving rapidly and we need to take advantage of this change. Likewise, our education system has also gone through a complete overhaul. But we also need to focus on improving learning outcomes through increased engagement and interactivity. So, we will keep coming with innovative solutions that can help make learning a fun activity.

A MESSAGE TO PARENTS

Books will always remain the best primary source of learning. Learning from videos is a new trend, but it's a passive activity. You will gain information faster, but retention will be much lower. Studies have shown that the human brain retains more information when gained over a long period. Augmented reality will help your child learn faster, while still keeping the books as the primary source of learning.
